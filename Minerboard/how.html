How it works

We created Minerboard to bring all benefits of professional mining to all people.

Minerboard is a unique software, that will allow you to manage and configure your mining devices, manage productivity and see full analitics of your mining activities at a given period of time.

Minerboard is distributed to private miners for free, and has a paid PRO version with comprehensive extended functionality aimed at managing large-scale mining operations.

Minerboard is currently being developed and tested. If you want to participate in closed trial period, please fill in the request form. Release of minerboard is scheduled for winter of 2018
